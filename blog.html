About FLower Shop

We love flowers, and it shows. For decades we’ve been the go-to flower shop for the citizens of India and beyond. We have often been voted Best in India when it comes to flower delivery. Our 100% satisfaction guarantee ensures that you, or the person you are sending flowers to, is happy with what they have received.


We’re quite proud of our local flower shop and what we’ve made of it over the years. We’d love it if you’d stop by, whether you’re interested in purchasing a floral arrangement or if you’d just like to browse and see some flowers you’ve never seen before. You can find us at karve Nagar,Pune in Maharashtra. Hope to see you soon!


Flower Delivery


You won’t be surprised to know that flower delivery is our primary form of business. Yes, it feels good to see the look on someone’s face when you send them flowers, but isn’t it also a great joy to get the message from them thanking you for sending them. Everyone loves getting flowers!


We’re happy to offer same-day flower delivery if you order by 9:00 to Maharashtra and cities like Pune, Nagpur, Mumbai, Hyderabad, Bangloure, Chennai,Madurai and Delhi.


We also deliver daily to many nursing homes, hospitals, churches, and funeral homes. We have quite a wide delivery area, so just click here to find out if your particular destination is in our delivery area. Of course, you can also send flowers long distance, as we’re part of a nationwide group of florists who work together to deliver flowers across the country.


Order By Phone Or Online


One of the great things about our online flower ordering option is that you can see exactly what we have. You can shop by occasion or product. At that point you can order online or just pick up the phone and tell us which one you’re most interested in. You can even just call us up and let us know what occasion you’re sending flowers for; we can send something along according to your suggestions or for your particular budget. The fact is, we can accommodate your needs, because we’re a flower shop that knows flowers better than anyone else around!
